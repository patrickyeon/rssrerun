Podcast Reruns

I'm not sure if this is how everybody else listens to podcasts, but I like to subscribe to a bunch, auto-download them to my phone, and then have my listening app always play me the oldest episode. Once I've subscribed to a podcast, it's all automatic from there on out. This breaks down when I find an older show and want to listen to the back-catalog as well. The usual pattern is to download the older episodes and either binge on them or manually interleave them into my ongoing listening. No longer! Choose a feed here, set a re-broadcasting schedule, and the rssrerun service will generate a new feed for you. Drop that into your podcatcher and it will let you catch up at a natural pace and just switch over to the "live" schedule once you're up to date.

Try it out

Paste in the URL of a podcast feed below, and the server will try to build a super-feed with all of the items it has ever had, and from there it will create a re-run for you. Not every podcast can be rebuilt back to its start, but we're working on it.
